Course Offered

COURSE DURATION ELIGIBILITY
BTech (Regular) 4 Years
  • Minimum 50-60 percent marks in Class 12 exams, which may vary depending on the college and the chosen specialization.
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics subjects in 12th grade. Biology students are also considered for admission to a few BTech specializations.
  • must have passed national, state, or university-specific entrance examination.(JEE,KEAM,etc..)
BTech (Lateral Entry) 3 Years
  • A Diploma in Engineering (or a related field) from a recognized institution. Minimum marks in the diploma program as per university requirements, which is typically around 50%
  • must have passed national, state, or university-specific entrance examination
MTech 2 Years
  • Requires a B.Tech/B.E. degree with a minimum of 60% marks (or 55% for SEBC, and a pass for SC/ST)
  • Selection criteria is a valid GATE score, with non-GATE candidates considered for any remaining seats
