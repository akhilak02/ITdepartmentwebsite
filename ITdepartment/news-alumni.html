NEWS AND ALUMNI

Alumni Association

The Government engineering college Idukki (GECI) starts functioning at Painavu, the Idukki headquarters, to impart technical knowledge in various fields of engineering. The campus is presently operating at kuyilimala, surrounded by scenic beauty and pristine forest. Since its inception in 2000, GECI completes 20 years of successfully generating graduates and postgraduates in various disciplines. Within 20 years, more than 5000 students have completed their graduate and postgraduate courses. The GECI alumni association functions in the college to build storing relation between the passed out students and the alma mater. The alumni association is in the process of preparing a database of GECI alumni. The students who have passed out from GECI can be a part of this alumni association by filling out the registration form. Request all of you to be a part of this process.
